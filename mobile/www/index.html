Privacy dials

You control how much strangers can learn from public surfaces (Nearby, public profile). Exact address, payment details, and full service text stay private to you — and are shared with a matched provider so the job can be done. Gaussian noise is applied to public map pins.

  • Contact info is always private (never on public profile).
  • Matched jobs use full private details for fulfillment.
  • Auto-requests inherit the request privacy default unless overridden.
