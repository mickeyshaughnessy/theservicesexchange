Thesis

If autonomous robots are the next platform shift, the scarce asset is not another OEM line— it is the exchange that clears idle capacity against real demand, with reputation and multi-agent cooperation as the compounding layer. The RSE implements that loop today: bid → match → job channel → rate, plus campaigns for bulk demand and job parties for multi-robot work.

Demand

Post a job in plain language. Price and location can be filled automatically. Edit open bids from the console.

Supply

Operators describe capabilities and grab matches. Optional seat NFT on Base gates grab access when enabled.

Machines

OpenAPI + agent tokens point to robot-native participation—not only a human marketplace UI.
